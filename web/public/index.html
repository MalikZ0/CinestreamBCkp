Features

For Users:

  • Registration and login functionality
  • Access to User-side User Interface
  • Ability to change profile settings
  • Ability to search, watch, purchase content
  • Ability to add reminders and view purchased ticket details
  • Ability to watch live-streaming and post-published content
  • Access to community page and engage with creators
  • Ability to change profile to creator profile if desired

For Creators:

  • Registration and login functionality
  • Access to creator-side User Interface
  • Ability to change profile settings
  • Can access everything same as users
  • Ability to live stream content
  • Ability to list future live event updates
  • Ability to upload content after streaming
  • Ability to sell e-tickets
  • Ability to place suitable ad-bars and in-ads
  • Ability to check real-time revenue and perform transactions
  • Ability to seek adverts and check previous activities with creators
  • Ability to create, develop, and manage community, and engage with audience through video chats and messages
